About TGO

Discover the story behind Taj's Graphical Overhaul and the passionate team working to transform your Satisfactory experience.

Our Mission

TGO was born from a simple yet ambitious vision: to elevate the visual experience of Satisfactory while maintaining the game's original artistic direction and ensuring optimal performance.

We believe that visual fidelity shouldn't come at the cost of playability, which is why we've spent countless hours optimizing every aspect of our modifications to maintain smooth gameplay across a wide range of systems.
